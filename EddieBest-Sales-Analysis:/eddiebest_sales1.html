EDA and Business Insight of Multi-Store Retail Sales: EDDIEBEST INT'L Ltd

EDDIEBEST INT'L Ltd is a Dubai-based computer and accessories retailer. They sell products like laptops, keyboards, webcams, printers, USB hubs,monitors, mice, headsets and related tech devices across various locations in Dubai like Dubai Mall,Al Barsha, Deira City Center and Marina Branch. They have provided a dataset that records 500 sales transactions including invoice numbers, product names, unit prices, quantities sold, store locations, payment methods, and transaction dates. However, this raw data remains underutilized without structured analysis and insights. To transform this data into actionable business intelligence, EddieBest has brought in a data science consultant. The goal is to uncover patterns, trends, and relationships within the sales data that can guide strategic decisions from pricing and inventory management to seasonal promotions and customer engagement strategies.

df["Date"] = pd.to_datetime(df["Date"], dayfirst=True)

df["DayOfWeek"] = df["Date"].dt.day_name()

Key Insights from EDA

  1. Al Barsha is the highest performing store. while Dubai Mall sees fewer but higher value purchases
  2. Webcam, Monitor, and Laptop lead revenue and sales volume
  3. Credit Card and Cash dominate payment methods
  4. Daily revenue trends show clear spikes, offering timing cues for promotions and restocking
  5. Sales peak during the months of December and September

Solutions

  1. rack multi product invoices for future bundling analysis
  2. Location Specific Promotions: Design targeted marketing campaigns for underperforming stores. Use the successful strategies of Al Barsha and Dubai Mall as models.
  3. Time Aware Promotions during peak months
  4. Promote Online Payments through app offers, loyalty rewards, or cashback incentives to improve customer experience and digital engagement
  5. Leverage price sensitivity insights by bundling low-cost accessories with high value items.
